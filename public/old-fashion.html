Old fashion

I personally love to drink old fashion after a good meal while is cold and raining outside .
In good company with some good relaxing, jazz,blues music in the back.
Is a nice drink that will stay with you durnig deep talks , a little sweet a little bitter and preatty dry so is very important to use your best bourbon or rye or any whiskey you like .
if you are intersted to know more about the story than check Here.

Method and preparation

Add first the sugar into the glass than angostura mix it well than add some ice ,
stir it up and garnish with a nice orange peel

Glass: Tumbler
Method: Build
Garnish: Orange peel

  • 5 cl bourbon whiskey
  • 1 cl suagar syrup / or 1 sugar cube
  • 5 dash angostura
