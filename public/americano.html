Americano

Americano cocktail is one of the most famous especially in Italy .
Bitter,herbal and sparkling , this drink is very popular before dinner less strong than the negroni and negroni sbagliato but is perfect to drink for an Aperitivo.
if you are interested to know more than check Here .

Method and preparation

Mix the red Vermouth and the Campari into the glass add some ice , stir it up and add soda then garnish with a nice Orange peel

Glass: Tumbler
Method: Build
Garnish: Orange peel

  • 3 cl Red Vermouth
  • 3 cl Campari
  • 3 cl Soda
