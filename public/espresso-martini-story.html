Espresso Martini

The origin of this drink, is not so long ago, The 1980s London.
Fred's Club, a young beautiful lady came to the bar she was probably a model, very beautiful.
It was friday night, the club vibe was pretty on fire and a long night was ahead the barman was Dick Bradsell.
He was pretty in a rush as always when the bar is getting busy.
Being behind the bar can be hell.
But something unexpected was happening that night Bradsell was focused on making drinks when a smell of nice perfume came trough his nose .
He looked up and there she was, a beautiful lady smiling at him , for a moment the hell became paradise .
He stopped for a second until she said "hey how you doing?."


Bradsell:

"hey,very good thank you !,How about you ?."


Young Lady:

"i am good,thanks but very tired it was a long day and a long night is still ahead i think i need a special drink tonight can you do that ?."


Bradsell:

"yes of course i can you talking to one of the best in the business!. tell me more, give me some input what do you like ?"


Young Lady:

"I think i need something strong but not too much, maybe with coffee something can wake me up and F***ks me up! ."
Bradsell Laughed for a second and thought for few more seconds untill he said :"ok i have it! lets try this."
In Italy it was and is, very famous the "caffe shakerato" .
That means shaken coffe so he probably knew that, and he also thought about another drink the Black Russian.
So he knew they could work together and decided to try so he made the espresso fast added 5cl vodka and 3cl kaluha shook all .
And there you go nice foamy coffee cocktail was ready.
when the lady tried it was so excited that she start shouting :"Wooow this is the best drink i have ever tried "".
