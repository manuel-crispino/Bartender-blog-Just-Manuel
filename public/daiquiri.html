Daiquiri

The Daiquiri is a White cuban rum based cocktail created in cuba simple but very nice and refreshing one of my preferite, i have always asked my self what is the difference between a rum sour and daiquiri no one can really answer my question so i come up with this recipe. many bars where i used to work where doing it like this and it make sense to me .
if you are intersted to know more than check Here .

Method and preparation

mix all the ingridients squize the lime and leave half lime shell into the Shaker add some ice ,shake it up 15seconds and garnish whith a nice lime peel or dry lime

Glass: coupette
Method: shake
Garnish: Peel or dry lime


  • 6 cl White cuban rum
  • 3 cl Fresh lime juice
  • 2 cl Sugar syrup/or/2-3 spoons caster
  • Half lime shell
