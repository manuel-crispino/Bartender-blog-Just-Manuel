Mojito

Mojito is a refreshing cocktail born in cuba who doesn't like Mojito? there are many many variants but i like the classic way refreshing little bit sour, soft to drink and a little sweet. by every sip you can feel your self in summer
if you are interested to know more than check Here .

Method and preparation

Start with 15 mint leafs add sugar and soda than muddle gently the mint add lime and Rum and stir with ice mae sure the mint will be going around all the surfaces going also up and down fill with ice add the Garnish and the straw .

Glass: Highball
Method: Build
Garnish: Mint and Lime

  • 4.5cl Cuban Rum
  • 2.5 cl Lime juice
  • 2cl Sugar Syrup
  • 15 Mint Leafs
  • 3cl Soda
