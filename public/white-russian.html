White Russian

White russian pretty easy cocktail just 3 ingredients there is also another variant without double cream call Black Russian.
Nice drink for who love Vodka based drink on ice little sweet well balanced from the coffee liquor slightly bitter and foamy on top .
if you are interested to know more than check Here .

Method and preparation

Mix Vodka and Coffee liqueur into the glass and stir it up with some ice .
Than shake the double cream and gently added on top using a spoon to help you.

Glass: Tumbler
Method: Build/ Double cream shake
Garnish: 3 coffee beans


  • 4.5 cl Vodka
  • 4.5 cl Coffee liqueur
  • 3 cl Double cream well shake
