Black Russian

Black russian pretty easy cocktail just 2 ingredients there is also another variant with double cream call White Russian.
Nice drink for who love Vodka based drink on ice little sweet well balanced from the coffee liquor slightly bitter .
if you are interested to know more than check Here .

Method and preparation

mix all the ingredients into the glass and stir it up with some ice .

Glass: Tumbler
Method: Build
Garnish: No garnish


  • 4.5 cl Vodka
  • 4.5 cl coffee liqueur
