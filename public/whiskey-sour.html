Whiskey Sour

Whiskey sour one of the best drinks very easy and refreshing.
All you need is 1 bottle of bourbon or rye whiskey,lemon, sugar and if you like egg white.

if you are intersted to know more than check Here.

Method and preparation

mix all the ingridients into the glass add some ice , stir it up and garnish whith a nice orange peel

glass: Tumbler
Method: Shake
Garnish: Orange peel

  • 5 cl Whiskey
  • 2.5 cl Lemon juice
  • 2 cl sugar syrup /or/2-3 spoons caster
  • 2 cl egg white optional
