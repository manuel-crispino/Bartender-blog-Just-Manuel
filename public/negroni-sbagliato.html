Negroni Sbaglaito

The negroni sbagliato is basically a mistake negroni created by mistake .
The creator was busy speaking with a guest and instead of using gin he accidentally poured Prosecco and serve it to the guest
The guest liked it and there you go negroni sbagliato is born.
The Sbagliato is nice to drink before dinner with classic aperitivo is bitter sweet bubbly and has this little acidity of the prosecco
This is why is so loved isn't strong like Negroni and is a bit stronger than the Americano.
if you are interested to know more than check Here .

Method and preparation

Mix vermouth and gin into the glass add some ice than gently add prosecco mix gently only one time using the spoon from bottom to the end , stir it up and garnish with a nice orange peel.

Glass: Tumbler
Method: Build
Garnish: Orange peel

  • 3 cl Prosecco
  • 3 cl Red Vermouth
  • 3 cl Campari
