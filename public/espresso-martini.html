Espresso Martini

The origin of this drink is not so long ago 1980s London, Fred's Club, a young lady come to the bar and asked the barman Dick Bradsell .
Young Lady:"Can you do something to wake me up and F***k me up ."
The drink is a coffee and vodka based cocktail a little bitter and low sugar the only sweet part comes from the liqueur .
The original recipe is whitout sugar but many bar like to add a bit of syrup like vanilla, choccolate ,honey or any others .
if you are intersted to know more than check Here .

Method and preparation

mix all the ingridients into the Shaker add some ice ,shake it up 10-15 seconds and garnish whith 3 coffe beans .

Glass: coupette
Method: shake
Garnish: 3 coffe beans


  • 5 cl Vodka
  • 3 cl coffee liqueur
  • 3 cl Espresso coffee
  • Sugar is optional or any syrups you like
