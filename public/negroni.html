Negroni

The Negroni cocktail was born in Florence and takes its name from Count Negroni. Legend says returning from a trip from London he went to his Usual bar ,and asked the Barman to mix the usual Campari and Vermouth with a little bit of gin brought from England. and there you go the Cocktail Negroni was created .
if you are interested to know more than check Here .

Method and preparation

Mix all the ingredients into the glass add some ice , stir it up and garnish with a nice orange peel

Glass: Tumbler
Method: Build
Garnish: Orange peel

  • 3 cl Gin
  • 3 cl Red Vermouth
  • 3 cl Campari
